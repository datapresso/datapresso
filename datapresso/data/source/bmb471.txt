But it also plays into the perpetrators' hands. As the details about the bombings in Boston unfold, it'd be easy to be scared. It'd be easy to feel powerless and demand that our elected leaders do something -- anything -- to keep us safe. If it's hard for us to keep this in perspective, it will be even harder for our leaders.
